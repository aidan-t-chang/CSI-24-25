Ordered, Unordered, and Definition lists: Cultural Diversity


What I think is a priority for cultural diversity in an environment:

  1. No discrimination against anyone for their culture
  2. Maintain respect for others no matter their culture
  3. Create a safe environment where people feel safe to share their culture

Some more priorities:

  1. Allowing all kinds of food to be allowed(in a classroom setting)
  2. Discourage splitting people by their culture
  3. Have events that showcase different cultures

More About My Favorite Foods:

Pork Dumplings
Dumplings filled with a mixture of pork and green onions, adding flavor.
Egg Custard Buns
Commonly eaten as a dessert, these are buns filled with a certain egg custard that yields a sweet and savory taste.
BBQ Pork Buns
Using the same buns as the egg custard buns, the BBQ pork buns are instead filled with, well, BBQ pork.
This has been one of my all time favorites for a very long time.
Sesame Balls
Also commonly eaten as dessert, sesame balls are a glutinous outside ball line with sesame seeds and a tasty paste on the inside.
