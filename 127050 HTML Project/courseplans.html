Survey Tutoring

This tutoring program will be a multi-person class where you will be open to ask questions about your computer science assignments and also learn the basics of computer science. The class will last 8 weeks, meeting once or twice a week and will require minimal amounts of time outside of sessions. This class will be taught in python and will go over the following topics:

Topic Length
Hello World in Python 1 Week
If, Else, Elif and Boolean Statements 1 Week
Getting Inputs From the User 1 Week
For loops and While loops 2 Weeks
Importing Libraries 1 Week
The Hashmap 2 Weeks

Data Structures and Algorithms Tutoring

These tutoring sessions will follow a very similar format to the survey tutoring, except for the fact that significantly more complicated topics will be learned and taught. The full course will be 8 weeks long, and the accelerated course will be 6 weeks long. The sessions will be taught in Python. We will meet twice a week and will require 1-2 hours of extra practice per week. The topics are as following:

Topic Length
Arrays and Hashmaps 1 Week
Two Pointers 1 Week
Sliding Window 2 Weeks
Stacks and Queues 1 Week
Linked Lists 2 Weeks
Binary Search 1 Week

1 on 1 Tutoring

The 1 on 1 tutoring will not follow a strict curriculum, but instead follow either the Survey Tutoring or the Data Structures and Algorithms classes, based on your choice. These sessions will be more personalized, allowing you to learn faster and understand concepts better. Because there is no strict curriculum, there is no set length of the course, instead going on as long as you want it to. If you have done a Survey Tutoring or Data Structures and Algorithms tutoring course, your first month of 1 on 1 tutoring will be completely free.
